My Girfriend's Secret Instant Noodles Recipe

Instant noodles are a quick and easy meal enjoyed worldwide. This recipe enhances the classic preparation by adding a drizzle of olive oil,
which enriches the broth with a smooth texture and a deeper, more satisfying flavor. The olive oil not only balances the seasoning but
also adds a subtle richness that elevates the taste of the dish.

With just a few simple ingredients and a short cooking time, this dish is perfect for a quick snack or a comforting meal. Feel free to customize
it with vegetables, proteins, or spices to suit your preferences. Whether enjoyed plain or with additional toppings, these enhanced instant noodles
will surely satisfy your cravings

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant noodles (any flavor)
  • 2 cups of water
  • Seasoning packet (included with the noodles)
  • Teaspoon of Olive Oil

Steps

  1. In a small pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Add the instant noodles to the boiling water. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally to separate the noodles.
  3. Turn off the heat. Pour the seasoning packet into the pot and stir well to combine.
  4. Very important!!! add the olive oil and ejoy!
